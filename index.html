Experience

Industry

My industry experience spans semiconductor manufacturing, advanced polymer development, and startup innovation. Through roles at GlobalFoundries, SABIC Specialty Plastics, and emerging healthcare ventures, I have applied materials science principles to solve real-world engineering challenges while collaborating across multidisciplinary teams.

Research

My research focuses on understanding the relationship between processing, structure, and performance in advanced materials. I have conducted characterization-driven investigations in polymers, composites, biomaterials, and metallurgical systems using techniques including SEM, XRD, GPC, CT imaging, and mechanical testing.

Projects

I enjoy transforming ideas into tangible engineering solutions through hands-on design and experimentation. From developing sustainable biopolymer composites to leading a nationally competitive bladesmithing team and designing assistive mobility devices, my projects combine technical rigor with creativity and practical problem-solving.

Leadership

Leadership has been a defining part of my engineering journey. Through organizations such as Material Advantage, the Society of Women Engineers, and Engineers Without Borders, I have built programs, secured funding, led multidisciplinary teams, and created opportunities that empower students and strengthen engineering communities.

Community

K–12 STEM Outreach

Engineering should be accessible, engaging, and inspiring for every student. Through programs at RPI and the Society of Women Engineers, I have led hands-on STEM experiences in materials science, engineering design, and scientific discovery. My outreach has reached hundreds of middle and high school students through lab demonstrations, workshops, mentoring, and Girl Scout initiatives. I am passionate about helping students see themselves as future engineers and researchers by combining technical learning with mentorship to make STEM approachable and impactful.

Volunteering & Service

Community engagement has been central to my college experience. Through Engineers Without Borders, Panhellenic Council, Alpha Gamma Delta, and other campus initiatives, I have supported projects in education, sustainability, philanthropy, and community development.
